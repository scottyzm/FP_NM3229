Climate change's hidden culprits

Although it's quick and easy to blame China, India and the USA for being the criminal masterminds behind the world's drastic climate change, a further analysis of the imports and exports of each of these countries exposes other nations involved. Through outsourcing and globalization, the physical producers of CO2 are not particularly the driving force behind climate change, but instead the nations generating exports are those at fault.
